## Ingredients

- 5 large potatoes
- 2 Tbsp. unsalted butter
- 125 ml heavy cream
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp. italian herbs
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 tsp. nutmeg

## Method

1. Peel and dice potatoes. Rinse in cold water, then boil, stirring occasionally, until they fall apart when poked with a fork.
2. A few minutes before the potatoes are done, heat the butter and cream in a sauce pan. Peel and crush the garlic, then add it along with the herbs and let it simmer for a few minutes.
3. Drain the potatoes and return to pot. Add the butter/cream/garlic mixture and the dry spices, then mash.

## Variations

- Add some freshly shredded cheddar cheese while mashing
- Use thin-skinned potatoes and leave the skins on
- Add some flavorful red spices like chipotle or smoked paprika
